FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking your self-drive car with Holiday Self Drive Cars.

What documents are required to book a self-drive car?
A valid driving license, government-issued photo ID (Aadhaar/Passport), and a refundable security deposit are required at the time of pickup.
How much is the security deposit?
The security deposit varies by vehicle category and is fully refundable upon a clean return inspection, subject to fuel and damage policy.
Is a minimum driving license validity required?
Yes, drivers must hold a valid license for at least 1 year, and be aged 21 years or above for hatchbacks/sedans and 23+ for SUVs/MPVs.
What is the fuel policy?
Vehicles are handed over with a specific fuel level and must be returned at the same level. Any shortfall will be charged at prevailing rates.
What is the cancellation policy?
Free cancellation up to 24 hours before pickup. Cancellations within 24 hours may attract a nominal charge — contact us for details.
Do you offer corporate or long-term bookings?
Yes — we offer flexible monthly and corporate rental packages with dedicated support. Call Mr. Harish directly to discuss a custom plan.
